Treemap Demo

Yipit UK Deal Mix

Viz Type: Treemap
Data: Dummy Data
Show Me: Our Deal Mix and Performance
Description: A visual representation of deal mix. Each rectangle in the treemap displays two values. The size of the rectangle reflects the number of deals run as a percentage of all deals run. The color of the rectangle shows the average performance relative to all deals run. The scale at the top of the chart shows you the range of available colors, the darker the rectangle, the higher the performance for that specific category. Click on a category to see the subcategories that comprise each category. Right click to go back (up) one level.
Visualization Justification: Treemaps simultaneously show the big picture, comparisons of related items, and allow easy navigation to the details.[1] Treemaps work with hierarchical data to visualize large chunks of information that would otherwise be difficult to visualize.
