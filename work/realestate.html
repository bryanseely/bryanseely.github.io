About Lue Spriggs Real Estate

Lue Spriggs is a fictional real estate agent created as a showcase for a eye-catching and impactful realtor website design. Lue is a Seattle area native and has lived in Ballard for over 25 years. Lue has experience buying and selling homes all over North Seattle and has seen the market through all its ups and downs. His intimate knowledge of the local market will ensure you get the best deal on you purchase or sale.

Lue has been recognized by his industry peers as one of the top agents in Seattle and has received awards from the local and national real estate communities.

Lue has two young daughters and stays involved in the Seattle community through school, sports, and dance recitals. Lue is passionate about housing for all and he volunteers at the United Way of King County, with his family, with a goal of ending homelessness in the Puget Sound region.
